PhD students Kusuyama, Takeuchi, and Miura presented their work in the poster track at [Augmented Humans 2026](https://augmented-humans.org/), held at OIST.
<!-- 詳細情報はmore以下に記載 -->
<!--more-->
This work is part of a collaborative research project conducted within the Osaka University Humanware Innovation PhD Program and the Next-Generation AI Talent Development Program, under the mentorship of Assistant Professor Amesaka. The project also involved Stephan Kingsbery, a special auditor student who visited the XR Group during the Fall/Winter semester of 2025.

+ Yoshihiro Yokoyama*, Stephan Kingsbery*, Masaki Takeuchi, Hiroki Kusuyama, Yuto Fukui, Kohei Miura, Seiya Mori, Takashi Amesaka, “A Pilot Study for Walking Direction Guidance using Subtle Blur Effects,” Augmented Humans 2026 Poster and Demo Track (4 pages), March 18, 2026.
+ Kohei Miura, Seiya Mori, Hiroki Kusuyama, Masaki Takeuchi, Yoshihiro Yokoyama, Yuto Fukui, Takashi Amesaka, “Preliminary Study on a Real-time CSF-based Blur Filter for Unobtrusively Saliency-modulated Augmented Reality", Augmented Humans 2026 Poster and Demo track (4 pages) (17 March 2026).
