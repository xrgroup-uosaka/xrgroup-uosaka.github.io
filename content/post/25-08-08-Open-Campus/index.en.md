We exhibited an XR photo booth at the Open Campus event of the School of Engineering Science, The University of Osaka.
<!-- 詳細情報はmore以下に記載 -->
<!--more-->
When visitors spread their hand near their face, the school’s mascot "Shigma" appeared through projection mapping, allowing them to take a photo with Shigma. The booth was planned and supervised by Professor Iwai, and implemented and demonstrated by second-year master’s students Konishi and Yamamoto.

A total of 76 high school students experienced the booth and took photos.
