PhD student Kusuyama received the [IEEE Kansai Section Student Paper Award](https://www.ieee-jp.org/section/kansai/award/studentlist.html).
<!-- 詳細情報はmore以下に記載 -->
<!--more-->
The award-winning paper is as follows:
+ Hiroki Kusuyama, Yuta Kageyama, Daisuke Iwai, and Kosuke Sato, “A Multi-aperture Coaxial Projector Balancing Shadow Suppression and Deblurring,” IEEE Transactions on Visualization and Computer Graphics (Proceedings of IEEE International Symposium on Mixed and Augmented Reality), Vol. 30, No. 11, pp. 7031-7041, 2024.
