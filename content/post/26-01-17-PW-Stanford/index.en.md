We delivered one invited talk (Assoc. Prof. Nakamura) and four oral presentations (PhD students Kusuyama, Miura, Sommart, Takeuchi) at [SPIE Photonics West 2026](https://spie.org/conferences-and-exhibitions/photonics-west), held in San Francisco, USA.
<!-- 詳細情報はmore以下に記載 -->
<!--more-->
During our stay, we also visited the [Stanford Computational Imaging Lab](https://www.computationalimaging.org/) led by Prof. Gordon Wetzstein, where we discussed potential future research collaborations.
***
- [Invited Talk] Tomoya Nakamura, Tomoyoshi Shimobaba, Yuichi Kozawa, Mohamad Ammar Alsherfawi Aljazaerly, and Tatsuki Tahara, “Coded Imaging under Natural Light via Incoherent Digital Holography and Lensless Optics,” SPIE Photonics West (Practical Holography XXXX: Displays, Materials, and Applications), 13917-23, San Francisco, California, USA, Jan. 2026.
- Hiroki Kusuyama, Tomoya Nakamura, Daisuke Iwai, “Coded aperture optimization for mitigating aberration in large-format Fresnel lens towards shadowless projection mapping,” SPIE Photonics West (Optical Architectures for Displays and Sensing in Augmented, Virtual, and Mixed Reality (AR, VR, MR) VII), 13821-43, San Francisco, California, USA, Jan. 2026.
- Masaki Takeuchi, Kohei Miura, Daisuke Iwai, “Effects of view-dependent highlights and specular intensity on gloss perception in projection mapping,” SPIE Photonics West (Optical Architectures for Displays and Sensing in Augmented, Virtual, and Mixed Reality (AR, VR, MR) VII), 13821-44, San Francisco, California, USA, Jan. 2026.
- Thanapong Sommart, Daisuke Iwai, “Online optimization of contrast and brightness of target images for radiometric compensation in projection mapping,” SPIE Photonics West (Optical Architectures for Displays and Sensing in Augmented, Virtual, and Mixed Reality (AR, VR, MR) VII), 13821-45, San Francisco, California, USA, Jan. 2026.
- Kohei Miura, Daisuke Iwai, “Exploring thermal contact potentials for Hand–Object Interaction refinement,” SPIE Photonics West (Optical Architectures for Displays and Sensing in Augmented, Virtual, and Mixed Reality (AR, VR, MR) VII), 13821-46, San Francisco, California, USA, Jan. 2026.
