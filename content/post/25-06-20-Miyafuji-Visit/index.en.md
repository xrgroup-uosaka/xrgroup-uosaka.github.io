**Title:** Spatial Augmented Reality and Human-Computer Interaction

**Abstract:** In this talk, I will introduce spatial augmented reality (SAR) using projection technology from the perspective of human-computer interaction (HCI). By leveraging projection, we can fuse digital information with the real world, thereby expanding the possibilities for new interactions that influence users' perceptions and behaviors. This lecture will also discuss practical applications that enhance human cognition and behavior, presenting a vision of future interfaces that integrate space and information.
