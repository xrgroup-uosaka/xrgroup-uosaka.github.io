## Join us
We are seeking highly motivated students who are enthusiastic about pursuing novel XR technologies that have the potential to revolutionize society.

#### Prospective graduate students
Please consult the [Admissions Assistance Desk (AAD)](https://www.osaka-u.ac.jp/en/international/inbound/dragongate). We do not accept research students. Only those who pass the entrance examination can join our lab. Most international students who join our lab are supported by a [MEXT scholarship](https://www.es.osaka-u.ac.jp/en/admission-aid/mext-granted/index.html) (Embassy recommendation).

#### Exchange programs
We accept students through [university-wide exchange programs](https://www.osaka-u.ac.jp/en/international/inbound/exchange_program/) (OUSSEP, iEXPO, FrontierLab, Maple).
