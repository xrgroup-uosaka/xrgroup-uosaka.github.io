<!-- 見出し文 -->
Two papers from the XR Group have received a Best Paper Award and an Honorable Mention at [IEEE VR 2026](https://ieeevr.org/2026/awards/conference-awards/).
<!-- 詳細情報はmore以下に記載 -->
<!--more-->
A paper by our alumnus Takahiro Okamoto and PhD student Masaki Takeuchi (in collaboration with Associate Professor [Masataka Sawayama](https://www.mswym.com/site/) from Hokkaido University) received the Best Paper Award, while a paper by M2 student Kotaro Fujimura and PhD students Hiroki Kusuyama and Masaki Takeuchi received the Honorable Mention Award.

---
**Best Paper Award**

Takahiro Okamoto*, Masaki Takeuchi*, Masataka Sawayama, Daisuke Iwai, "Shadowless Projection Mapping for Tabletop Workspaces with Synthetic Aperture Projector," IEEE Transactions on Visualization and Computer Graphics (Proceedings of 2026 IEEE Conference on Virtual Reality and 3D User Interfaces (VR)), 2026.
