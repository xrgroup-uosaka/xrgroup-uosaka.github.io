The XR Group provided full support for the organization of the international conference on AI and XR, [IEEE AIxVR 2026](https://aivr.science.uu.nl/2026/), held from January 26–28, 2026, at Sigma Hall, a facility of the Graduate School of Engineering Science, The University of Osaka.
<!-- 詳細情報はmore以下に記載 -->
<!--more-->
Professor Iwai served as the Financial Chair. Six doctoral students (Kusuyama, Taninaka, Miura, Sommart, Sinaei, and Takeuchi), one master’s student (Maeda), and two undergraduate students (Kishi and Tahara) contributed as student staff members to support the conference operations.
